  • Bitcoin: Created in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto. Bitcoin was the building block for all other coins to follow. It was designed to not need a central authority and was considered to be run by the public. The coin is not tied to any real-world entities but tied to address. Owners of the coins cant be identified due to the cryptography within transactions in the public block chain.

  • Litecoin: Created in 2011 by Charlie Lee. Litecoin is considered to be a fork in Bitcoin core by decreasing block generation time within the ledger (2.5 min , Bitcoin is estimated at 10 min) . Increased maximum amount of coins, and contains a different hash-algorithm.
