The birth of Cryptocurrencies began in 2009 when Satoshi Nakamoto wanted to make a decentralized currency that was intended not to be regulated by Governments. Since 2009 thousands of coins have arose and these coins are known as altcoins.

Cryptocurrencies are a form of digital currencies, that pose as an alternative to current currencies. Each cryptocurrency uses cryptography to secure its transactions, verify transfers, and control the amount of coins to be created.

Cryptocurrencies formulated the technology known as the block-chain. The block-chain is a public database where ALL transactions related to the specific cryptocurrency are added to keeping a running ledger. The key behind this block-chain technology, is that USERS have access to “the books”, Users can see ALL transactions, giving validity to the public. The block-chain is managed by a peer to peer network (Miners) to validate each block, or transaction.

Mining, on a general note, is ordinary people using their computer resources and electrical power to solve mathematical equations known has hashing. Each transaction block contains a hash ID and miners job is to make sure person A is actually sending money to person B. After the Miner validates the transaction the Miner is given a small fee in cryptocurrency for their services.
